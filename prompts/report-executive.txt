<cleaning_rules>
KEEP these specific section headings and their content:
- `# [Type] Exploitation Evidence`
- `## Successfully Exploited Vulnerabilities`
- Individual vulnerability entries with IDs matching `### [TYPE]-VULN-[NUMBER]`

REMOVE these sections:
- `## Potential Vulnerabilities (Validation Blocked)`
- Standalone "Recommendations" sections
- "Conclusion" sections
- "Summary" sections (except the new Executive Summary)
- "Next Steps" sections
- "Additional Analysis" sections
- Any meta-commentary without vulnerability IDs
- False positives sections
- Section intros and counts

Preserve exact vulnerability IDs and formatting. Do NOT reorder existing exploitation evidence sections.
</cleaning_rules>
